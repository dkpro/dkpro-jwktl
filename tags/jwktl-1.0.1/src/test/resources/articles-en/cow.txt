====Noun====
{{wikipedia}}
{{en-noun|pl='''[[cows]]''', '''[[cattle]]''' ''or'' '''[[kine]]''' (''archaic'')}}

# A female domesticated [[ox]] or other [[bovine]], especially an adult after she has had a [[calf]].
# More generally, any domestic bovine regardless of sex or age.
# The [[meat]] ([[beef]]) of such animals, used as [[food]] for [[human]]s.
# The [[female]] of larger species of [[mammal]], including bovines, [[moose]], [[whale]]s, [[seal]]s, [[hippo]]s, [[rhino]]s, [[manatee]]s, and [[elephant]]s.
# {{context|British|derogatory|informal}} A [[woman]] who is considered despicable in some way, especially one considered to be fat, lazy, ugly, argumentative, mean or spiteful.
# {{informal}} Anything that is annoyingly difficult, [[awkward]] or [[graceless]].
#: ''That website is a real '''cow''' to navigate.''
# {{informal}} A [[conniption fit]] or [[hissy fit]]; a state of [[agitation]] {{context|only in the phrase '''have a cow'''}}.
