Cognate with {{etyl|nl|-}} {{term|baars||baars|perch, bass|lang=nl}}, {{etyl|de|-}} {{term|Barsch||perch|lang=de}}. More at {{l|en|barse}}.

====Noun====
{{en-noun|es|pl2=bass}}

# The [[perch]]; any of various marine and freshwater fish resembling the perch, all within the order of [[Perciformes]].

=====Usage notes=====
The plural '''bass''' refers to multiple fish of a single variety or species, whereas '''basses''' refers to multiple varieties or species.
